### History Taking

Key elements include:
- Onset and progression
- Associated symptoms
- Past medical history
- Medication history
- Family history

### Investigations

**Imaging:**

1. **Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT)**
   - RNFL thickness
   - Ganglion cell analysis
   - Optic disc parameters

2. **Neuroimaging**
   - MRI brain and orbits
   - CT for bony evaluation
   - Angiography when indicated

**Laboratory Studies:**
- Blood tests based on suspected etiology
- Inflammatory markers
- Nutritional assessment
