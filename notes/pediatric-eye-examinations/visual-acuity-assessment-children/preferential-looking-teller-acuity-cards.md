# Preferential Looking and Teller Acuity Cards

## Introduction and Overview

This comprehensive clinical note covers the essential aspects of preferential looking and teller acuity cards, a fundamental topic within Visual Acuity Assessment in Children. Understanding this subject is crucial for optometry students preparing for licensing examinations (DOH, DHA, MOH, NBEO) and for clinical practice.
