
# Treatment: Laser Photocoagulation and Anti-VEGF

## Introduction

Management of Eales Disease requires a comprehensive approach that addresses both ocular manifestations and systemic disease control. This review outlines treatment options, management strategies, and coordination of care for patients with Tuberculosis and Eye.

## Treatment Goals

### Primary Objectives

1. **Prevent vision loss**: Primary goal of all interventions
2. **Stabilize disease**: Prevent progression
3. **Restore function**: When possible
4. **Manage complications**: Address acute issues
5. **Optimize systemic control**: Address underlying disease

### Treatment Hierarchy

Management typically follows a stepwise approach:

| Priority | Intervention | Indication |
|----------|-------------|------------|
| First-line | Primary treatment | Standard cases |
| Second-line | Alternative options | Incomplete response |
| Adjunctive | Combined approaches | Complex cases |
| Rescue | Emergency intervention | Acute complications |
