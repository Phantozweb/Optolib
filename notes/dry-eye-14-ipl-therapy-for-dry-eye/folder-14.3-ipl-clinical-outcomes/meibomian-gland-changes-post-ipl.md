# Advanced MGD Interventions

## Thermal Pulsation Therapy (LipiFlow)

### Mechanism
The LipiFlow system applies controlled heat to the inner eyelid surface (42.5°C) while simultaneously applying pulsating pressure to the outer eyelid surface. This combination liquefies obstructed meibum and expresses it from the glands.

### Treatment Protocol
1. **Pre-treatment Assessment**
   - Meibography to assess gland status
   - Symptom questionnaire
   - TBUT measurement
   
2. **Procedure**
   - Topical anesthetic applied
   - Eyepiece inserted under eyelids
   - 12-minute treatment cycle
   - Patient remains comfortable throughout

3. **Post-treatment**
   - Immediate improvement possible
   - Full effect in 4-6 weeks
   - Repeat treatment every 6-12 months

### Clinical Outcomes
- Improves meibum quality
- Increases TBUT
- Reduces symptoms
- May prevent further gland loss
- 79% of patients show improvement

## Intense Pulsed Light (IPL)

### Mechanism
IPL delivers broad-spectrum light (500-1200 nm) that:
- Targets abnormal blood vessels (telangiectasia)
- Reduces inflammatory mediators
- Improves meibomian gland function
- May affect Demodex mites

### Treatment Protocol
1. **Preparation**
   - Apply cooling gel
   - Use protective eye shields
   
2. **Treatment Parameters**
   - 3-4 sessions, 2-4 weeks apart
   - Energy settings based on skin type
   - Lower lid treated primarily
   
3. **Post-treatment**
   - Avoid sun exposure
   - Meibomian gland expression often combined

### Clinical Outcomes
- Reduces telangiectasia
- Improves meibum quality
- Reduces symptoms
- Effects last 6-12 months

## Intraductal Meibomian Gland Probing

### Mechanism
Sterile probes are inserted into meibomian gland orifices to break fibrotic adhesions and open obstructed ducts.

### Technique
1. Topical anesthesia
2. 1-2 mm sterile probe inserted
3. Gentle probing to release adhesions
4. May feel "pops" as adhesions release
5. Multiple glands treated

### Indications
- Obstructive MGD refractory to other treatments
- Palpable gland obstruction
- Glands with potential for recovery

### Clinical Outcomes
- Immediate symptom relief
- May restore gland function
- Repeat treatments may be needed
- Minimal complications

## Clinical Pearls for Licensing Exams

1. LipiFlow temperature is 42.5°C for 12 minutes
2. IPL targets telangiectasia and inflammation
3. Probing breaks fibrotic adhesions
4. These treatments are for refractory MGD
5. Combination therapy often most effective
