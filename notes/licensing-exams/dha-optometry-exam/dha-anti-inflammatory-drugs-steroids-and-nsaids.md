# DHA Anti-inflammatory Drugs - Steroids and NSAIDs

## Introduction

Ocular pharmacology is critical for the DHA Dubai examination.

## Drug Classes

| Class | Use | Examples |
|-------|-----|----------|
| Anti-glaucoma | IOP reduction | Latanoprost, Timolol |
| Anti-inflammatory | Reduce inflammation | Prednisolone, Ketorolac |
| Anti-infective | Treat infections | Moxifloxacin, Acyclovir |
| Anti-allergy | Manage allergies | Olopatadine |
| Mydriatics | Dilation | Tropicamide |

## Anti-Glaucoma Medications

### Prostaglandin Analogues

| Drug | Dosing | Side Effects |
|------|--------|--------------|
| Latanoprost | Nightly | Iris darkening |
| Travoprost | Nightly | Similar |
| Bimatoprost | Nightly | Hyperemia |

### Beta-Blockers

| Drug | Dosing | Contraindications |
|------|--------|-------------------|
| Timolol | BID | Asthma, bradycardia |
| Betaxolol | BID | Fewer pulmonary effects |

## Steroids

### Potency Ranking
1. Difluprednate (most potent)
2. Prednisolone acetate 1%
3. Dexamethasone 0.1%
4. Fluorometholone 0.1%
5. Loteprednol (least potent)

### Side Effects
- Elevated IOP
- Cataract progression
- Delayed healing
