# DHA Contact Lens Complications - Hypoxia and Infection

## Introduction

Contact lens practice is significant for the DHA Dubai examination.

## Materials

### Soft Lens

| Material | Properties |
|----------|------------|
| HEMA | Low water |
| Silicone hydrogel | High oxygen |

## Fitting

### Base Curve Selection

| Curvature | Base Curve |
|-----------|------------|
| Flat (< 41 D) | 8.6 mm |
| Average (41-45 D) | 8.4-8.6 mm |
| Steep (> 45 D) | 8.2-8.4 mm |

### Assessment Criteria

1. Movement: 0.2-0.5 mm
2. Centration: Covers limbus
3. Comfort: Good
4. Vision: Clear

## Complications

| Condition | Signs | Management |
|-----------|-------|------------|
| Edema | Thickening | Higher Dk |
| Neovascularization | Vessels | Discontinue |
| Ulcer | Infiltrate | Urgent referral |
