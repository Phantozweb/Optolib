# DHA Question-Taking Strategy - MCQ Approach

## Introduction

Success in the DHA Dubai examination requires strategic preparation and test-taking skills.

| Week | Focus Area | Daily Hours |

| 1-4 | Foundation building | 3-4 |

## Question Analysis

### The PACE Method

**P** - Read the Prompt carefully
**A** - Analyze key information
**C** - Consider all options
**E** - Eliminate wrong answers

## Time Management

| Section | Time Allocation |
