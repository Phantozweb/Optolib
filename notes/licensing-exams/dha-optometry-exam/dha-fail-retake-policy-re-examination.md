# DHA Fail & Retake Policy - Re-examination

## Overview

Understanding the passing criteria for the DHA Dubai optometry licensing examination is essential for effective preparation.

## Passing Score Requirements

| Examination Component | Minimum Score | Maximum Score |
|----------------------|---------------|---------------|
| Written Examination | 60-65% | 100% |
| Clinical Assessment | 70% | 100% |
| Overall Combined | 65% | 100% |

## Score Report Interpretation

### Components of Score Report

1. **Total Score:** Overall performance percentage
2. **Section Scores:** Performance by topic area
3. **Pass/Fail Status:** Clear indication of result
4. **Percentile Rank:** Comparison with other candidates

| Domain | Score | Pass Threshold | Status |

## Re-examination Policy

### Waiting Periods

| Attempt | Waiting Period |
|---------|---------------|
| 1st Failure | 3 months |
| 2nd Failure | 6 months |
| 3rd Failure | 12 months |
| 4th+ Failure | 12 months + remedial course |

## License Renewal Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| CPD Hours | 30-40 hours annually |
| Mandatory Topics | Ethics, patient safety |
| Documentation | Maintain records for 5 years |

## Appeals Process

| Step | Timeline | Action Required |
|------|----------|-----------------|
| 1 | Within 14 days | Submit written appeal |
| 2 | 7 days | Receive acknowledgment |
| 3 | 30 days | Review committee decision |

## Success Strategies

1. Comprehensive study plan
2. Regular self-assessment
3. Mock examination practice
4. Clinical skill refinement
