# INTERNATIONAL Professional Ethics - Code of Conduct

## Practice Guidelines for International Optometry

### Scope of Practice

- Comprehensive eye examinations
- Refraction and prescribing
- Contact lens fitting
- Diagnostic testing

### Prescribing Rights

- Topical medications
- Anti-infectives
- Anti-inflammatories
- Mydriatics

### Referral Criteria

| Condition | Urgency |
|-----------|---------|
| Glaucoma | 2 weeks |
| Retinal detachment | Same day |
| Orbital cellulitis | Emergency |
