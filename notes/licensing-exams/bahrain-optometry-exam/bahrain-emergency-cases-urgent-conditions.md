# BAHRAIN Emergency Cases - Urgent Conditions

## Clinical Cases for Bahrain NHRA

### Case 1: Diabetic Retinopathy
**Patient:** 55-year-old diabetic
**Findings:** Microaneurysms, hemorrhages, exudates
**Diagnosis:** Non-proliferative diabetic retinopathy
**Management:** Ophthalmology referral, glycemic control

### Case 2: Red Eye
**Patient:** Contact lens wearer
**Findings:** Pain, redness, corneal infiltrate
**Diagnosis:** Microbial keratitis
**Management:** Discontinue lenses, urgent referral

### Case 3: Pediatric Amblyopia
**Patient:** 5-year-old with anisometropia
**Findings:** Reduced vision in one eye
**Diagnosis:** Anisometropic amblyopia
**Management:** Glasses, patching therapy

### Case 4: Glaucoma Suspect
**Patient:** 60-year-old with family history
**Findings:** IOP 24 mmHg, suspicious disc
**Diagnosis:** Glaucoma suspect
**Management:** Visual fields, OCT, monitoring
