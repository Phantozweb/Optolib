# MOH Good Standing Certificate - Application

## Introduction

This guide outlines steps after passing the MOH UAE examination.

## License Application

### Steps

| Step | Timeline | Action |
|------|----------|--------|
| 1 | 7 days | Receive results |
| 2 | 30 days | Submit application |
| 3 | 2-4 weeks | Processing |
| 4 | Approval | Receive license |

### Required Documents

- Valid passport
- Examination pass certificate
- Degree certificate
- Good standing certificate
