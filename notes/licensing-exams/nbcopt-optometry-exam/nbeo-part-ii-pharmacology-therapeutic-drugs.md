# NBEO Part II Pharmacology - Therapeutic Drugs

## Pharmacology for NBCOPT USA

### Anti-Glaucoma Medications

| Class | Mechanism | Examples |
|-------|-----------|----------|
| Prostaglandins | Uveoscleral outflow | Latanoprost |
| Beta-blockers | Decrease production | Timolol |
| Alpha-agonists | Both mechanisms | Brimonidine |

### Anti-Inflammatory Agents

- Steroids: Prednisolone, Dexamethasone
- NSAIDs: Ketorolac, Bromfenac

### Anti-Infectives

- Fluoroquinolones: Moxifloxacin
- Aminoglycosides: Gentamicin
- Antivirals: Acyclovir

### Mydriatics

| Drug | Duration |
|------|----------|
| Tropicamide | 4-6 hours |
| Cyclopentolate | 24 hours |
