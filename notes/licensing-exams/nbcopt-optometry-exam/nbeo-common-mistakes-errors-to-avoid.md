# NBEO Common Mistakes - Errors to Avoid

## Common Pitfalls in NBCOPT USA

### Question-Answering Errors

1. Not reading entire question
2. Missing qualifying words
3. Second-guessing correct answers

### Clinical Reasoning Errors

1. Premature closure
2. Anchoring bias
3. Confirmation bias

### Time Management Errors

1. Spending too long on one question
2. Not reviewing flagged questions
3. Leaving questions unanswered

### Prevention Strategies

- Practice timed tests
- Systematic question analysis
- Trust clinical training
