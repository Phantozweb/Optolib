# NBEO Clinical Reasoning Method - Systematic Thinking

## Examination Strategy for NBCOPT USA

### Time Management

- Allocate 1.5-2 minutes per question
- Flag difficult questions
- Reserve time for review

### Question Approach

1. Read entire question
2. Identify key information
3. Eliminate wrong answers
4. Select best option

### High-Yield Topics

- Glaucoma
- Diabetic retinopathy
- Refraction
- Contact lenses
- Pharmacology

### Last-Week Tips

- Review summaries
- Practice timed tests
- Rest adequately
