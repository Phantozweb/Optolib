# NBEO Re-examination Policy - Retake Procedures

## Passing Requirements for NBCOPT USA

### Minimum Scores

| Component | Pass Threshold |
|-----------|---------------|
| Written | 60-70% |
| Clinical | 70% |
| Overall | 65-70% |

### Score Interpretation

- **Pass:** Above minimum threshold
- **Fail:** Below threshold
- **Conditional:** May need specific sections retaken

### Re-examination Policy

| Attempt | Waiting Period |
|---------|---------------|
| 1st fail | 3 months |
| 2nd fail | 6 months |
| 3rd fail | 12 months |

### Score Validity

- Valid for 2-3 years
- May require re-examination if expired
