# OMB Eligibility Requirements - Qualifications

## Overview

This guide covers registration for the Oman Medical Board optometry licensing examination.

## Eligibility Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Primary Degree | Bachelor's degree in Optometry or equivalent |
| Accreditation | Recognized optometry program |
| Clinical Hours | Minimum supervised clinical hours |

## Registration Steps

1. Create account on official portal
2. Submit application with required documents
3. Pay examination fee
4. Schedule examination
5. Receive confirmation

## Documentation Required

- Degree certificate and transcripts
- Good standing certificate
- Professional references
- Identity documents
- Medical fitness certificate

## Fee Structure

| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Application | Variable |
| Examination | Variable |
