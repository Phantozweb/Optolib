## Practice Questions for Saudi SCFHS

### Question 1
A 55-year-old patient presents with gradual vision loss. IOP is 24 mmHg, optic disc shows cupping with C/D ratio 0.7. The most likely diagnosis is:

A. Ocular hypertension
B. Primary open-angle glaucoma
C. Normal tension glaucoma
D. Angle-closure glaucoma

**Correct: B**

### Question 2
The mechanism of action of latanoprost is:

A. Decrease aqueous production
B. Increase trabecular outflow
C. Increase uveoscleral outflow
D. Pupillary constriction

**Correct: C**

### Question 3
Which of the following is a contraindication for timolol?

A. Diabetes
B. Asthma
C. Hyperopia
D. Dry eye

**Correct: B**

### Question 4
In pediatric refraction, the recommended cycloplegic agent is:

A. Tropicamide
B. Phenylephrine
C. Cyclopentolate
D. Atropine (for infants)

**Correct: D**

### Question 5
A contact lens wearer presents with a red, painful eye. The most important immediate step is:

A. Prescribe antibiotic drops
B. Discontinue lens wear
C. Change to new lenses
D. Use lubricating drops

**Correct: B**
