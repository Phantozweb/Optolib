# SCFHS Eligibility Requirements - Qualification Criteria

## Overview

This guide covers registration for the Saudi SCFHS optometry licensing examination.

## Eligibility Requirements

| Requirement | Details |
|-------------|---------|
| Primary Degree | Doctor of Optometry (OD) or equivalent |
| Accreditation | Accredited optometry program |
| Clinical Hours | Minimum requirements vary by jurisdiction |

## Registration Steps

1. **Create Account** on official portal
2. **Submit Application** with required documents
3. **Pay Examination Fee**
4. **Schedule Examination** at approved testing center
5. **Receive Confirmation** and prepare

## Documentation Required

- Degree certificate
- Academic transcripts
- Good standing certificate
- Professional references
- Identity documents

## Fee Structure

| Fee Type | Amount |
|----------|--------|
| Application | Variable |
| Examination | Variable |
| Re-examination | Additional fee |

## Important Deadlines

- Registration deadline: Typically 30-60 days before exam
- Late registration: May incur additional fees
- Document submission: Before registration deadline
