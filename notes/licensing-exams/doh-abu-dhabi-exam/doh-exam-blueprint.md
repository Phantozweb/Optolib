# DOH Exam Blueprint & Weightage

## Examination Blueprint Overview

The Department of Health (DOH) Abu Dhabi optometry licensing examination follows a structured blueprint that ensures comprehensive assessment of competencies required for safe and effective optometric practice in the UAE.

## Content Domain Distribution

### Primary Domains

| Domain | Weight (%) | Questions | Description |
|--------|-----------|-----------|-------------|
| Clinical Optometry | 25% | 25 | Refraction, examination techniques, diagnosis |
| Ocular Disease | 30% | 30 | Pathology recognition, management, referral |
| Ocular Pharmacology | 12% | 12 | Drug mechanisms, prescribing, interactions |
| Contact Lenses | 12% | 12 | Fitting, complications, care systems |
| Binocular Vision | 8% | 8 | Strabismus, amblyopia, vergence disorders |
| Pediatric Optometry | 5% | 5 | Childhood vision disorders, development |
| Low Vision | 3% | 3 | Assessment, devices, rehabilitation |
| Public Health & Ethics | 5% | 5 | Screening, epidemiology, professional standards |
